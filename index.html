Now Push

Now Push - bridging the gap between your phone, tablet and computer.

Share, Notify, Remember

Share text, links, and other files between all your devices. Send yourself reminders with "Remind me in ..." so you never forget anything.

Public Threads

Create and Follow Public RSS feeds in Now Push to create "Threads" within the app. Get notifications for your Threads.

Private Threads

Create Private Threads to have your own person feeds and use them to integrate with Zapier or IFTTT.

Secure Data

Now Push is a secure platform with full "End to End Encryption" for all messages by default, no exceptions.
